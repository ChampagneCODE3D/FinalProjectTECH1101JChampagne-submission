Eddie The Eagle Biography
"I could not see during 30% of my jumps" - Eddie the Eagle

Ski Jumping Career Record


International Competition Results

Eddie the Eagle continued to train and compete after the 1988 Calgary Olympics. He attempted to qualify for the 1992 Olympics in Albertville, France. He also tried for the 1994 Olympics in Lillehammer, Norway, but was unsuccessful. Although Eddie was able to improve his jumping distances, he fell short of the new standards that were implemented after Calgary.
