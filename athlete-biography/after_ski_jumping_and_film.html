Career After Ski Jumping

After he retired from ski jumping, Eddie became a ski instructor and actually achieved 9th spot in the world for speed skiing. He is a motivational speaker and a Patron of the charity Ski 4 Cancer, returning to ski jumping at age 52 for the cause. Eddie's story has inspired many across the world. His story was also depicted in the movie "Eddie The Eagle" released in 2016 by Lionsgate.
