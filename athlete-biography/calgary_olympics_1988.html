Eddie The Eagle Biography
"I only did two 120m ski jumps before Olympic debut" - Eddie the Eagle

Calgary Olympics 1988


Fan Favourite

Eddie was well liked by the crowds in Calgary during the 1988 Olympics. They were elated just to see him complete a landing. The underdog captured the hearts of many watching, both in person and around the world.
